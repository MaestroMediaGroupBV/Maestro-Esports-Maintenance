About Maestro

Maestro is a value driven, focused and ambitious esports organisation based in the Netherlands, taking the industry by storm.





Who we are

What is Maestro

Maestro Esports is an electronic sports organisation, home to some of the most-skilled gamers from around Europe. Maestro aims to give players a platform in which to hone their skills and develop a professional approach to competitive gaming.

Our Organisation

History

Maestro Esports has been established in early 2014 as a group of friends, with a common interest in gaming. However, after a reevaluation of Maestro's future plans, Maestro Esports then transformed into a gaming organization in early 2015. After building the organisation over the years, Maestro Esports has become a registered entity in the Commercial Register of Amsterdam under Maestro Media Group B.V. in November 2017.
