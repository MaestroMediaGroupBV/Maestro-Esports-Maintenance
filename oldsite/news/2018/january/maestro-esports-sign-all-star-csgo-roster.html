Maestro Esports sign all star CS:GO roster

Amsterdam based organisation Maestro Esports have signed the former Vanguard Esports team. The Benelux all star team ended their contracts with Vanguard Esports a few weeks ago. Months prior they were champions at SugarLAN 2017 a regional LAN tournament held in Belgium. As well as the BNC - Belgian National tournament and gained qualification into the ESL Pro Benelux League.

After a successful 2017 and tweaks to the starting five, the revitalized team felt Maestro was the right choice for their future in esports.

In-game leader and AWPer, Bryan “Bolin” Kesteloot stated:

“We are proud to announce that our new line up will be a part of Maestro Esports. Our team is very motivated and pumped to represent Maestro in the biggest CS:GO events in the Benelux. We are glad to have found an organization that shares the same passion and determination for esports as our team which is not always easy to find in the Benelux scene. It’s also great to see that our scene has been growing a lot lately and we believe that a professional organisation like Maestro will only have a positive influence. We are thrilled to start this new chapter, and we will do our absolute best to mark Maestro as one of the best teams in the Benelux region.”
The Counter-Strike: Global Offensive roster consists of:
  • Bryan “Bolin” Kesteloot - IGL/AWP
  • Nigel “nGL” Deloof - Lurker
  • Lucas “SPARKEy” Winkels - Entry fragger
  • Nasrat “Infernal” Shafaq - 2nd/Entry support
  • Dennis “Hntr” Caluwe - Support

    • With such big signings of quality players, this is what Maestro CEO, Alessio Florio had to say:

    “We are all proud to have such committed and dedicated players. The potential for the lineup is huge, really looking forward to see what they can do.”

    Maestro Esports are extremely excited to see what the new team will bring, it’s a fantastic kick-off to 2018.
