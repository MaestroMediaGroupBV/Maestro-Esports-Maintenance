Elina “diva” Jacobsen re-joins Maestro Esports

Former rifler diva has re-joined Maestro Esports as an Assistant GFX Designer

The switch comes after the Norwegian featured in the Maestro Esports female CounterStrike: Global Offensive team. Elina Jacobsen signed for the team in early 2017 where she played a vital role in the roster. Accompanied by her Swedish teammates the 17 year old hit the ground running, performing well in a whole host of scrimmages.

She went on to showcase her talents during Copenhagen Games 2017 the team’s first LAN event.

However due to personal issues, Elina decided it was best to resign from her position at Maestro Esports shortly after the tournament. The teenager felt it was best to focus on school and in particular her graphic design studies. Elina completed a wide range of professional designs in her spare time and whilst in school. In a conversation with the staff at Maestro, Incorporated the Norwegian’s talents for design were discovered.

After already establishing a friendly relationship with each staff member. Elina went on to accept a position at organisation as an Assistant Graphic Designer.

The former CS:GO rifler stated:

“I’m very happy to be back, this time as a staff member. It’s always a positive and sociable atmosphere. Now I get to do something I love, still within the organisation that I love.”

Elina Jacobsen will now be part of the design team in a mutual agreement with us. Maestro, Inc is extremely happy to have the young designer onboard to aid in her growth and personal development.
