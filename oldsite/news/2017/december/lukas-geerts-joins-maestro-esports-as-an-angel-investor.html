Lukas Geerts joins Maestro Esports as an angel investor

Dutch CounterStrike: Global Offensive caster invests into Maestro Esports.

Lukas Geerts also known as “HardGlitcherzz” within the CS:GO scene expressed keen interest in joining the organisation. Maestro first worked with Lukas back in the summer of 2017 where he was contracted to cast the ESL Female Open League. Since then, after building a strong relationship with the Maestro staff, he saw Maestro Esports as an organisation he wanted to be a lot more involved in.

After working with Maestro for many months now, Lukas stated:

“I joined Maestro because they saw something in me when I started casting professionally. Other than that I do love the vision that Maestro has and the people behind it. Everyone at the organisation is so friendly and I love the direction the executives are taking the business in. Additionally it’s a big plus joining them as I am Dutch and they are growing so rapidly within and outside of the Benelux scene.”

When he isn’t casting matches, Lukas also works as the Head Administrator for the Mijndomein Masters a CS:GO league broadcasted by Fox Sports. The Dutch caster will now sign as an angel investor owning equity in the organisation and is eager to help drive Maestro forward.

At Maestro Esports we are very specific about who we choose to be a part of our management team. We are thrilled to have Lukas onboard with us because we felt his long-term ambitions aligned with ours.
